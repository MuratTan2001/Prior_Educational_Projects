Where To Begin

You must be new to carving. You might have even bought the tools. However without guidence, carving can be especially dangerous. We hope you have taken the precoutions needed. To help you on your journey and protect you from possible mistakes you might make we advise you take a look at our site in Skillshare!

Develop Your Skills

As a veteran carver, you might think that you have learned everything. However carving is a very general term, discovering new techniqes, mastering different kinds of whittling and cutting we promise you won't leave with dissapointment. Come visit our youtube channel where we make all kinds of woodcraft!

Share Your Work

If you are a aspiring wood artist, we welcome you to share your work and join our community. You can join competions and compete with people all over the world. Even if you haven't become famous yet, contact us! We will help support you. Come visit our gallery, find insparation and take advice by the creators themselves!
