We are a community

We were established in 1997 with a passion for carving. Starting from Harvard Houston's Annual Carving Gathering From countryside to urban life, we grew to become a well known community of artists. Since our founding, we are proud that we supported countless artists. We hold annual gatherings, help other people in their projects and hold competitions.
